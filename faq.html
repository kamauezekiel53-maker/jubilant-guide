Frequently Asked Questions

Is MP3Juice free?

Yes, the MP3Juice platform provides free music search and audio conversion tools.

Do I need an account?

No. All features are available without registration.

Where does the music come from?

Search results aggregate from public audio sources and videos based on user queries.

Why does some audio fail to download?

Certain tracks may be restricted or removed from public hosting sites.

Is MP3Juice safe?

Yes. MP3Juice does not store files; downloads come directly from supported external hosts.
